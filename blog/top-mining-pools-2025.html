Factors to Consider When Choosing a Pool

  • Fee Structure: Lower fees improve your overall earnings.
  • Payout Method: PPS and FPPS offer consistent payouts, while PPLNS may vary.
  • Server Locations: Closer servers reduce latency and stale shares.
  • Reputation: Established pools like ViaBTC and F2Pool have proven reliability.

Which Pool is Best for You?

If you're mining **Bitcoin**, ViaBTC and F2Pool remain top choices due to their reliability. For **Litecoin and Dogecoin**, LitecoinPool is excellent because it offers **0% fees** and merged mining with DOGE.
